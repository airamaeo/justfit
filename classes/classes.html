All our classes are free with a JustFIT membership. Equipment is cleaned before and after use.

All JustFIT Members have access to JustFITPLUS which is an Online Portal packed full of all your favourite online classes on-demand! Take a look here.

STEP

A classic is back – Step. This choreographed session will let you ‘step up a sweat’. You’ll burn calories and shape your body with lots of jam-packed fun and enjoyable dance moves. Come along to step up and down to some of your favourite tunes.

YOGA

In a JustFIT Yoga class you will be guided through a sequence of physical postures promoting flexibility, mobility, stability, and strength. Book in today with our fully qualified instructors.

KETTLEBELLS

Kettlebells is all bells, no whistle. A hardcore workout, that focuses on technique, safety and quality of movement. This class will work every major muscle, build power and shred body fat, JustFIT Style.

ASS & ABS

Does this class really need a description? We will strengthen and tone those all important areas! We will use a variety of equipment including barbells, dumbbells and kettlebells.

PUMP

Pump is one of the best ways to get in shape and tone up as it challenges all of your major muscle groups while you squat, press, lift and curl. A fun class with great benefits and also a great introduction to weight training.

PILATES

JustFIT Pilates is based off Joseph Pilates classical mat exercises. Following the 6 principles of Pilates, concentration, control, centre, flow, precision and breathing; this class is a full body and mind workout. Each class will focus on mobility, stability and strength. This ensures the body is balanced, reducing the risk of injuries in daily life and sports.

MOBILITY

You’ve never stretched like this before! Addressing all areas of movement & mobility with elements of yoga, flexibility training & self myofascial release (foam rolling). Relieve tight sore muscles, improve your joint ROM & prevent injuries. Use your body with ease & freedom!

SPIN

JustFIT Spin is not just an indoor cycling class but an indoor cycling experience. Let the energy and music move you. This class will take you on a journey through tough climbs and fast sprints. The ultimate calorie burning class that will have you racing to the finish line!

STRONG

Strong is the perfect class for those who want to gain more functional strength through the use of dumbbells kettlebells and other equipment. The JustFIT coaches will bring you through a balanced strength training session correcting form and positions throughout! Our plan is to give you all you need to turn yourself into the strongest version of you!
